About Us

Capturing Moments • Creating Stories • Building Careers

Professional photography focuses on precision, lighting, and composition to create high-quality images for various purposes. It requires a strong command of technical skills and creative control.

Our Story

HRK School of Photography is one of the premier photography institutes in India, located in Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala. We believe that "What you learned & How you learned" is more important than "Where you learned". Therefore, we have designed a comprehensive syllabus that maximizes the value of your time and equips you with essential skills for a successful photography career.

Our program emphasizes practical experience alongside theoretical knowledge, ensuring that your learning is efficient and avoids unnecessary content that can be found on YouTube or Google. We focus on industry-relevant skills that prepare you for real-world photography challenges.

Meet Our Mentor

Hari R Krishnagadha

Our esteemed mentor, Hari R Krishnagadha, is a professional photographer with 7 years of experience in commercial photography and 5 years of teaching experience. His exceptional ability and dedication ensure that students grasp even the most challenging concepts of photography and lighting.

Specializing in product and portrait photography, Hari R Krishnagadha offers students the opportunity to learn the best techniques and insights from his extensive experience. Many students have learned photography under his guidance and are successfully pursuing their careers in the field.
